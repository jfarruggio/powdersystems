D120 Bag Filler


The D120 Bag Filler is a filling and weighing machine for USDA dairy applications. It produces (2) 50lb - 25kg bags per minute.

Specifications

  • Production Capacity
    Up to (2) 50lb - 25kg per minute
  • Filled (NET) Weight Range:
    From 20 - 100lbs Standard
    (Option for drum & Bag-In-Box)
  • Air Supply
    3 CFM @ 80 PSI
  • Electrical
    460V 15A, 3PH
  • Controls
    Allen-Bradley
  • Dimensions
    112" ± 3" H x 56" W x 47" D
    2050 lbs
Optional Items
  • Dust Collection
  • Access Platform
  • Rare Earth Magnet
  • Deaeration Probe

Deaeration Probe


Used to remove air from product prior to bagging allowing for more product per cubic foot in each bag.

  • Works in conjunction with the D120 Bag Filler
  • Relativ Humidity can NOT exceed 45% ± 5% at temperatures below 65℉
    • Some producs, such as whey and caseinates, require the relative humidity to be below 38% (35%

Bag Kicker

  • Welded 3-4 Stainless Steel Construction

PSD120 Product Settling Device



The PSD120 Product Settling Device helps to settle product in the bag while also openig the bag for better product flow. Not all powder products perform the same while being bagged. The temperature and relative humidity of the air in the Fill Room can affect product flow into the bag. The PSD120 Product Settling Device helps to eliminate some of the variables.

Features
  • Completely Automatic
  • Adjustable actuation time
  • Maintenace Friendly
  • Sanitary Design
  • Gentle on the bag
